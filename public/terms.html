2. About CEC Nepal and Our Services

Clean Energy Consultants Pvt. Ltd. (CEC Nepal) is a VAT-registered engineering consultancy firm established in 2006 in Nepal. We operate under our brand identity CEC Nepal and our guiding motto: "Clean Energy for Generations." Our principal office is located at Kathmandu, Nepal.

Our professional services include, but are not limited to:

  • Hydropower project planning, pre-feasibility and feasibility studies, and detailed engineering design

  • Solar energy system design and renewable energy consultancy

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), Initial Environmental Examination (IEE), and environmental monitoring

  • Construction supervision, quality control, and project commissioning

  • Topographical survey, LiDAR survey, hydrological study, sediment analysis, and geological investigation

  • Electromechanical and hydromechanical equipment design and procurement advisory

  • Transmission line design, substation planning, and power evacuation systems

  • Physical and numerical modelling of hydraulic structures and hydropower components

  • Preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPR), tender documents, and bankable feasibility reports

  • Engineering training programs, capacity building workshops, and applied research

  • Water resource development, river management, and irrigation consultancy

It does not constitute an offer of professional engineering services.
