Who We Are?
Clean Energy Consultants Pvt. Ltd. (CEC), established in 2006, stands as a leading national consultancy firm with a sharp focus on planning, feasibility studies, detailed engineering design, construction monitoring, and commissioning of hydropower, solar, environment, and infrastructure projects. We actively confront the evolving challenges faced by developers and investors at all project stages, turning these challenges into opportunities for impactful solutions. Our team combines extensive expertise and insights to deliver sustainable outcomes that add significant value to hydropower and solar initiatives, water resource development, environment and tunneling, river management, and other critical infrastructure projects.

Vision: 
Clean and green energy generation and effective water resource management for a sustainable future and shared prosperity.

Mission: 
Transform emerging challenges in hydropower, solar energy, green energy, water resources management, environmental sustainability, and infrastructure projects into opportunities.
